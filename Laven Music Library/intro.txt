Laven is a music library tool for exploring numerous artists' songs, biographies and albums by the use of API.
(API used: TheAudioDB)

Features:
- Search bar to search for artists (located at bottom left corner)
- Navigate through artist's tracks by clicking 'Next' or 'Previous' 
  button
- 'Shuffle' button to randomise a track
- 'Play' button to open up the current track's YouTube music 
  video
- 'Biography' button to know the Biography of the Artist
- 'Album' button to showcase a list of Albums of the Artist
